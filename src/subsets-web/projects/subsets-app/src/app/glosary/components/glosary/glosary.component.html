Glossary

The various indicators used and calculated in this tool are described in the tables below.


Generic indicators


{{ header }}
Drought stress Number of water stress days Number of days per month, for which the ratio of actual to potential evapotranspiration (ERATIO) is below 0.5. The ERATIO is computed following a simple water balance model as in Jones and Thornton (2009). ndws Days
Flooding stress Number of days with water logging Number of days per month, for which the soil is at least 50% between field capacity and saturation. It is computed using the same simple water balance model as for NDWS. ndwl Days
Flooding stress Extreme daily precipitation The 95th percentile of precipitation per month, computed using daily data. p_95 mm

Heat stress Number of days with high VPD The count of days per month for which VPD is equal or greater than 4 kPa. High VPD can lead to reduced stomatal conductance, and lower transpiration and photosynthesis. NVPD4 Days

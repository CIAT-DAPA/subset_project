Glossary

These tables contains the information related with the different indicators calculated in this tool.

Generic indicators

{{ header }}
Number of water stress days Number of days per growing season, for which the ratio of actual to potential evapotranspiration (ERATIO) is below 0.5. The ERATIO is computed following a simple water balance model as in Jones and Thornton (2009). days
Number of days with water logging Number of days per growing season, for which the soil is at least 50% between field capacity and saturation. Computed using the same simple water balance model as for NDWS. days
Extreme daily precipitation The 95th percentile of precipitation per growing season, computed using the daily data. mm
Total precipitation Total daily precipitation per month, computed using the daily data. mm
Average precipitation Average daily precipitation per growing season, computed using the daily data. mm
Average solar radiation Average solar radiation per month. W/m²
Average maximum temperature Average maximum temperature per growing season. °C
Monthly mean daylength Daylength of the middle Julian day of month per growing season. Hours
Number of days with high VPD The count of days per month for which VPD is equal or greater than 4 kPa. High VPD can lead to reduced stomatal conductance, and lower transpiration and photosynthesis. Days
Average VPD Average per growing season VPD. Kpa
Consecutive dry days Maximum number of consecutive dry days (P less to 1 mm), per growing season. days

Genebank collection subsetting tool

This Subsetting tool aims at generating custom subsets of multiple crops, accessions and environments. The identification of the subsets is based on the passport data available in Genesys, and on other indicators of temperature, precipitation, solar radiation, texture, soil, etc. Therefore, this tool will allow both germplasm requestors and genebanks to identify relevant and useful accessions to request or to distribute, respectively.

Methodologies

DBSCAN

Density-Based Spatial Clustering of Applications with Noise

HDBSCAN

Hierarchical Density-Based Spatial Clustering of Applications with Noise

HAC

Hierarchical Agglomerative Clustering
